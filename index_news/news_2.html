Manhattan Cocktails Handwritten Font

Say hello to Manhhatten Cocktails! - A ligature-based, casual handwriting script typeface, with a natural & lighthearted feel.

Make sure you have your Opentype Ligatures set to on in the app you are using , to get the most natural look for this script

If your app doesn't support Opentype Fonts - Manhattan Cocktails is PUA encoded - so you can still use Fontbook ( Mac) and Charmap( Windows) to copy and paste the ligatures you are after onto your typeline in any app you use.
