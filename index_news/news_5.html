Goops Font Introducing Goops! A fun Y2K-inspired handwritten bubble font in two styles, regular and bouncy! I mixed a bit of texture and a bit of goopy-ness to create a wonderfully imperfect and unique font. Plus, this font includes alternates for extra handmade goodness!.

Also great for logos, quotes, art, branding and more! Enjoy!
